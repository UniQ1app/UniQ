# 3.6 Identity Hub (Meritocratic CV)

Tracks Merit Score, wallet, and role.

## Merit Score Formula
Merit Score = Σ(Activity XP * Role Multiplier * Stake Modifier)

- Integrates wallet & staking for high-level roles
- Proof-of-Holding affects governance: selling tokens reduces Merit Score
