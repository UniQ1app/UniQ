# 4. Roles & Hierarchy

| Role         | Requirements     | Responsibilities                | Rewards               |
|--------------|-----------------|--------------------------------|---------------------|
| Cadet        | Free entry       | Learn, earn XP/Dust             | No withdrawal rights |
| Challenger   | Master Gate pass | Relay info, earn Merit          | XP/Dust             |
| Miner        | Stake required   | Produce content, execute missions| UNIQ & XP           |
| Computor     | High stake       | Validate content, govern        | High UNIQ, Merit    |
| Arbitrator   | Linked to Qubic  | Oversight & governance          | Role tied to Qubic  |
