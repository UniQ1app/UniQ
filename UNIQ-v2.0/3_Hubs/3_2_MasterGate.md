# 3.2 Master Gate (Filter & Exam Hub)

Meritocratic filter and economic entry point.

## Features
- Exams unlock advanced roles (Challenger, Miner, Computor)
- AGI Module: Dynamic Difficulty Adjustment (DDA)

### DDA Mechanism
Price_Difficulty = Base_Level * (Current_Computors / Active_Users)

- Automatically adjusts cost and difficulty to maintain scarcity and competence

## Role Unlocking
- Challenger → Relay info, earn Merit
- Miner → Requires Stake, produce value, earn UNIQ & XP
- Computor → High Stake, validate info, govern
